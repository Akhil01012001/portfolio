Analysis of World Population

  • Tech Stack: To effectively carry out the analysis, I have employed SQL as a primary tool. SQL (Structured Query Language) is a powerful language specifically designed for managing and querying relational databases. With its capabilities, I have able to manipulate and retrieve data efficiently, enabling us to perform in-depth analysis and generate meaningful reports.

In this project, I have actively engaged in analyzing and utilizing data sourced from the CIA World Factbook. The CIA World Factbook serves as a comprehensive compilation of statistical information pertaining to countries worldwide. Through this project, I have delved into various aspects of the data and extracted valuable insights. By leveraging SQL and the extensive dataset provided by the CIA World Factbook, I've strived to uncover valuable insights and contribute to the field of data analysis.
